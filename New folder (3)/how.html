How we work

We create advertisments that can be easily adapted by your team to suit your changing needs.

  1. You provide us with the information for what your advertising campaign is about.
  2. We use the information you have given us to create an advetising plan and then we meet with you to see how well it reflects your Company and your advertising ideas.
  3. Together we fine tune your advertising strategy and complete the initial advertisement.
  4. Once the advertisment strategy meets your needs and approval we run the advert on all relevant platforms.
  5. We can train your team to manage the advertisment sites or we can maintain them for you.
  6. Your advertisements can be easily adapted to suit your ongoing needs.
  7. As a client we think of you as part of the HashingAdvertisements family and will make it our responsibility to follow-up with you on a regular to see how we can enable you to make full advantage of all the advertising you require.
